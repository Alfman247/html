ISAs

An ISA can be extremely useful if you are trying to keep your money out of the hands of the taxman! Unfortunately, the majority of these do not provide interest rates nearly as great as a stand Easy Access account. Where I find these accounts really make a difference is with a Stocks and Shares ISA, as these are often the easiest and simplest investmeny accounts from most banks. I do not deem myself nearly qualified enough to talk about investments, so all I can really suggest would be to look at the past preformance of any accounts that take your fancy. Unfortunately, it is near impossible to predict the stock market, so, just do your reasearch first
