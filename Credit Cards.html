Credit Cards

Beginner and Basic Credit Cards

In the world of begginer or basic credit cards, there is no one card which I can reccomend this is due to the fact that the best begginer card is the one which you will get accepted for, as being declined will damage your credit score even more at the time where it is most fragile. I would reccomend using a wesbite such as MoneySuperMarket to help you compare and find what you will get accepted for. The same rule applies for basic credit cards, accept for the fact that it is probably wise to go with a card with your current bank, as this will make your life a bit easier. This goes alongside the fact that basic credit cards offer little-to-no rewards.

Reward Credit Cards

Reward Credit Cards are where things can get a bit more interesting. You still should complete eligibility checks, but you have way more options to compare between for different benefits. Most of these cards are free, but some may come with a small £25 annual fee, which in many cases is definitely worth it! This market is dominated by American Express, who offer amazing benefits with the catch of not being accepted at all stores.
